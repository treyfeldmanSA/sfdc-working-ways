A New Way to Build

Moving from chaos to clarity with two-week sprints

The Why: Acknowledging Our Challenges

Our current four-week sprint process isn't working. We're missing deadlines, scope creeps in uncontrolled, and teams are burning out trying to hit moving targets.

We need a better rhythm.

A Focused Cadence

We propose a Thursday-Wednesday sprint cycle to protect our weekends and improve focus.
